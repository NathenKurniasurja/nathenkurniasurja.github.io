In this post, I am trying to find out how team performance change throughout the season (better known as form). To do this I tried implementing the ELO system which changes each game depending on the result. I split performance up into attack and defence and plot the ELO over the 2024/25 season.

It seems that besides a few exception around December, most teams have an overall gradual change in performance. This makes it reasonable to assume future performance based on recent ones. It is however imperative to mention that the changes in performance for the discrete gameweeks are not smooth, hence the trend does not apply to every gameweek in the incline/decline.

The original reason behind this analysis, is that I was looking to find out if I could save up free transfers for a predictable form change. However, from the plots it looks like the duration of how a form lasts are too random to predict (too many factors play a part).
